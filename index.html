About Us

Mile High Motherplants is a Denver-based clone nursery inspired by the cosmic Mothership. We combine state-of-the-art cultivation practices with playful space themes to deliver healthy, vibrant plants. Our goal is to make top-shelf genetics accessible to growers while celebrating Colorado's pioneering cannabis culture.
